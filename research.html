Research Interests

My current interests include:

  • Continuous optimization;

  • Mixed-discrete and continuous nonconvex programming;

  • Large-scale optimization methods for machine learning.

My long-term interests include:

  • Convex analysis, large-scale decomposition, networks, game theory;

  • Algebra, computational algebraic geometry, cryptography;

  • Statistics.
