Tianyi Lin

 

Welcome to my homepage!

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Department of Mathematical Sciences at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. My advisor is Michael I. Jordan. I am also associated with Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR) and RISELab. Previously, I obtained my M.S in Pure Mathematics and Statistics from University of Cambridge and B.S. in Mathematics from Nanjing University.

My research interests include algorithms, optimal transport and game-theoretical learning arising out of questions in machine learning.
