Yibo Xu

 

Welcome to my homepage!

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Department of Mathematical Sciences at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. My supervisor is Dr. Yangyang Xu. I got my Ph.D. from Clemson University in 2018 under the supervision of Dr. Warren Adams. Previously, I obtained my M.S in Pure Mathematics and Statistics from University of Nevada, Reno, and B.S. in Mathematics from Nankai University.

My research interests include algorithms, optimal transport and game-theoretical learning arising out of questions in machine learning.
